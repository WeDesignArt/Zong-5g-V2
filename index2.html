Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any call set-up charges for nationwide offers?

No, there are no call set-up charges for nationwide offers.

How can I check bundle usage details?

You can check usage via MyZong App or dial *102#.

What is out-of-bundle rate?

Out-of-bundle rates apply once your bundle is consumed.

When will the usage notification messages be sent?

Notifications are sent when 80% and 100% usage is reached.

When will the Default Package be charged?

Default Package will be charged on the 1st of every month.

How can I activate International Roaming (IR) services on my number?

You can activate IR services by visiting your nearest Zong Customer Service Center.
